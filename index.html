Govt. Model Sr. Sec. School Sector – 33-D, Chandigarh is a prestigious school under the Department of Education UT Chandigarh holding its affiliation with CBSE. It was built in 1987 when the city beautiful was budding with limited educational facilities. Although it was a challenging course yet the diligent staff left no stone unturned and at present; the school is imparting education to approx. 2000 students.
The school is situated in the lap of nature, very near to terrace garden. The infrastructure is backed up with a separate Nursery
Section for the tiny tots and a well-equipped Junior Wing. We offer Medical, Non-Medical, Humanities and Skill Courses. The school has extensive playgrounds   well equipped laboratories in various disciplines and a well-stocked library holding nearly approx. 2000
books and subscription of newspapers and magazines. There is a Social Science, Science and Mathematics Park promoting
understanding through observation, an Eco-Club with a Greenhouse highlighting the importance of nature. The school strives to develop the various facets of child Personality through quality education .We Encourage children to take responsibility and develop a sense of respect for society & social cause. Thus year by year the students show excellence not only in academics but also in co-curricular activities. Their growth is stimulated in a safe and conducive environment. Their talents are channelized in a fruitful manner and they become versatile in life.
